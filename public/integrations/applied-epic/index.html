How it works

YOVU Communicator embeds as a context-aware panel beside account and activity workflows in Applied Epic.

📞

Screen pop

When a call comes in, the caller’s name, number, and Epic client record surface instantly — before you pick up.

👆

Click-to-call

Click any phone number in Applied Epic to dial directly. No copying numbers, no switching to a phone app.

📝

Activity logging

Calls, voicemails, and texts are automatically logged to the client’s activity timeline in Epic.

Why teams care

Faster quote follow-up, cleaner callbacks, fewer mistakes, and stronger recordkeeping — all without leaving Epic.

⚡

Faster response

Screen pop eliminates manual lookup. Teams answer with context in seconds instead of minutes.

Cleaner records

Stronger documentation
