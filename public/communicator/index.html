YOVU Communicator

The embedded workspace for calls, context, and follow-up.

YOVU Communicator brings call control, client context, recordings, transcription, AI summaries, and searchable history into Applied Epic and other browser-based workflows.

What YOVU Communicator is

YOVU Communicator is the client interface for the YOVU platform. It runs as a browser-based workspace that can float inside Applied Epic and other browser workflows, so users can manage calls and client context without leaving the page they are already in.

How it behaves in the workflow

Communicator lives inside the browser window your team already works in — resizable, dockable, and always in context.

Screen pop on ring

Caller identity and prior context surface the moment the phone rings — before the conversation starts. The team member knows who is calling and what they last discussed.

Dockable & resizable panel

Pin Communicator beside Applied Epic or any browser workflow so call controls never cover your work. It remembers where the user places it, and can shrink out of the way or stay visible as needed.

Warm transfer & conference

Drag-and-drop transfer to any colleague, directory presence shows who is available, and one-click conference to resolve calls faster without leaving the interface.

What users can do in one place

Everything a team member needs — call control, history, directory, logging, transcripts, summaries, and playback — in one interface.

Full call control

Answer, hold, transfer, conference, and end calls without switching windows. Pause and resume recording when sensitive information is discussed.

Click-to-call

Click any phone number in Applied Epic or the browser to dial — no manual dialling or copy-paste between systems.

Auto-logged activity

Calls, voicemails, and texts are automatically captured and tied to the client record in Applied Epic.

AI summaries & transcription

Every conversation is transcribed and summarized so notes are consistent and searchable. Sentiment analysis and topic tags highlight what matters.

Searchable call history

Find any past conversation by client name, phone number, date, or keyword across the entire team.

Recording playback

Replay any call directly from the history panel — for coaching, dispute resolution, or quality review.

Reception and team coordination

The same interface expands into a reception-style view with drag-and-drop controls for transfer and conference workflows.

Standard user view

  • Compact sidebar panel
  • Personal call controls
  • Activity logging & history
  • AI summaries & transcripts

Reception view

  • Expanded multi-line layout
  • Drag-and-drop transfer & conference
  • Queue and hold indicators
  • Team presence at a glance

Mobile continuity

Road-to-desk workflow — mobile users take recorded calls away from the office and records flow back into brokerage systems automatically.

1 Call on the road

Make or receive a business call from YOVU Mobile using the brokerage number.

2 Recorded & transcribed

The call is automatically recorded and transcribed — just like a desk call.

3 Synced to Communicator

Recordings, transcripts, and AI summaries appear in Communicator when the user is back at their desk.

4 Logged to Applied Epic

Activity is tied to the client record in Epic — no manual entry required.
