How it works in Epic

Communicator lives beside the activity and account workflow in Applied Epic, stays context-aware of what the user is viewing, and helps capture the communication record where the account already lives.

YOVU Communicator opens as a floating, resizable panel alongside Applied Epic. It knows which account or activity the user has open, so screen pop, call logging, and history are always tied to the right client. No app switching, no manual lookup, no lost context.

Core workflow modules

Six capabilities that change how your team works inside Applied Epic every day.

1. Click-to-call

Click any phone number in Applied Epic to dial directly. No copying numbers, no switching to a phone app. Outbound calls are logged automatically to the client's activity timeline.

2. Screen pop

When a call comes in, the caller's name, number, and Epic client record surface instantly before you pick up. The team member knows who is calling, what account they are on, and what they last discussed.

3. Activity logging

Calls, voicemails, and texts are automatically logged to the client's activity timeline in Epic. No manual entry required. Every interaction is captured.

4. Call history

A searchable record of every call: who called, when, how long, and what was discussed. Find any past conversation by client name, phone number, date, or keyword.

5. Recording playback

Replay any call directly from the history panel. Use recordings for coaching, dispute resolution, quality review, or E&O documentation.

6. AI transcription, summaries & sentiment

Every conversation is transcribed and summarized automatically. Sentiment analysis flags calls that need attention. Topic tags make conversations searchable.
