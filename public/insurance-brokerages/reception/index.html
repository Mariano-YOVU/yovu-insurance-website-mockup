Front-desk pain points

Reception handles the first impression for every caller — and the most volume in the brokerage.

❓

Unknown callers

Without screen pop, reception has to ask who is calling and manually look up the account — slowing every interaction.

🔀

Blind transfers

Without presence and warm transfer, calls bounce to voicemail or the wrong desk, frustrating clients and wasting time.

📝

Manual logging

Writing down caller details and messages by hand creates gaps in the record and delays follow-up.

Reception workflows with YOVU

Presence, transfer, and directory tools support confident first interactions.

Screen pop on ring

👥

Directory & presence

Warm transfer & conference
