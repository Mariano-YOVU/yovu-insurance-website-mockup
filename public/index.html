What YOVU is

Three products, one platform — built to work where your team already works.

YOVU Communications Platform

The parent product for calling, routing, mobile, recordings, reporting, and integrations. Everything your brokerage needs in one Canadian-supported platform.

YOVU Communicator

The embedded workspace inside Applied Epic and browser-based workflows — call control, context, history, transcripts, and AI summaries in one panel.

YOVU Mobile

Keeps teams connected on the road with recorded business calls, SMS, and voicemail that flow back to the office record automatically.

Built for the brokerage, not just the phone line

From reception to renewal, every conversation connected

New business calls, service inquiries, claims conversations, renewal follow-ups — YOVU connects them all to the client record in Applied Epic. Faster response times, cleaner handoffs, fewer manual steps, and documentation your team can actually rely on.

Whether your team handles 50 calls a day or 500, the workflow is the same: the phone rings, context appears, the conversation is captured, and the record is complete.
